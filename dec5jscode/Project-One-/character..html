The 7 Sisters: characters

Sydney Larson

This fiery redhead may seem like a hothead (figuratively, speaking) but she is actually quite nice, and the voice reason in her gang. Her powers include many different variants of pyrokenesis

Andrea Amante

A bit standoffish and undoubtly immature; This tall woman appears to be something of a loose cannon. But like almost all the girls in the gang, there's more to her than what's on the surface.

Mac Bellfree

If I were to tell you that some members of the gang were the embodiment of joy. Would you buy it? Well the same can be said Mackenzie Bellfree!

Madison Valeg

Small in stature, but quick, and light, on her feet. this little of joy is the little bit glue this keeps the gang together. kinda like Mr poopybutthole (Rick and Morty)

Maria Ford

One of the last original members, Maria seems pretty silent most of the time....very few know why

Mars folk

A slender, well educated literal mother. Mars is without a doubt the smartest member of the gang. Sadly this makes her a target to most villains
